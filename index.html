How to Use This Protocol

Step 1: Download

Select your guide type, tooth number (FDI), and bone parameters below. Download the .txt file.

Step 2: Import

Import the coordinates into your CAD software (Mimics/SolidWorks) to generate the guide spline curves.

Step 3: Fabricate
